Masseria Maresca

Situated in Ostuni, 5 km from Costa Merlata, Masserie Maresca features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. Free private parking is available on site. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Enjoy a cup of coffee from your terrace or balcony. Superior rooms have a spa bath or a hot tub. The farm stay also offers bike hire. The nearest airport is Brindisi - Salento Airport, 29 km from Masserie Maresca.

Masseria Santella Luxury B&B

Masseria Santella Luxury B&B offers accommodations in Cassano delle Murge, 20 miles from Bari Cathedral and 21 miles from Basilica of Saint Nicholas. 19 miles from Bari Central Train Station and 20 miles from Petruzzelli Theatre, the property provides a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a garden. The bed and breakfast features rooms with air conditioning, free private parking, and free Wifi.
